Albert Einstein

The Scientist who think and discover for the World.

Here's a time line of Albert Einstein:

  • Albert Einstein is a celebrated scientist in the 20th century. He’s famous for his discovery of the Theory of Relativity. And he paved the way for a branch of physics.

    • Here are other reasons his greatness is worth celebrating:

  • Free thinking philosophy – He was a genius. And he spoke with thoroughness and eloquence on a range of important humanitarian issues.
  • Flexible adviser – He advised Franklin Roosevelt about the power of nuclear physics. But later on, he advised against it.
  • Relentless determination – Although his educators thought of him as dim-witted, he never doubted his ability to learn. And so, he ventured into independent learning and taught himself math.

    • Another fascinating thing about Albert Einstein is his ambitious spirit. On his own, he made significant scientific discoveries, published prominent works, and won the Nobel Prize in Physics (1921). With all that he is, you could see why Michael Hart considers him the 10th influential person.
